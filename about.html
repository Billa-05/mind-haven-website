🌿 About Mind-Haven

Technology with empathy

Mind-Haven.uk is an emerging concept focused on creating emotionally intelligent digital support experiences. The idea behind the platform is simple: technology should not only solve problems, it should also understand people.

💛 Why Mind-Haven exists

Modern life connects people digitally but often leaves them emotionally isolated. Many individuals carry anxiety, loneliness, stress, and quiet emotional struggles that are rarely spoken about. Mind-Haven.uk is being imagined as a gentle, privacy-respecting digital companion that offers calm, support, and emotional understanding.

👩 Founder

Mrudula Billa — Founder of Mind-Haven.uk

Mrudula Billa is a UK-based founder with a background in Artificial Intelligence, software development, and ethical digital innovation. Mind-Haven.uk was founded with a clear vision to develop privacy-first, AI-driven emotional wellness solutions that are responsible, accessible, and human-centred. Her work focuses on building non-clinical emotional support technologies powered by conversational AI and intelligent sentiment analysis. She prioritises transparency, data privacy, and trust as the core foundation of the platform. Through Mind-Haven.uk she aims to contribute to the UK’s digital innovation ecosystem while advancing responsible AI in emotional wellbeing.

💛 Technology should care about people
