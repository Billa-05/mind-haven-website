🔒 Privacy

Privacy matters
at every step

Mind-Haven.uk is being developed with a privacy-first approach. Emotional wellbeing is deeply personal, and any future platform experience must respect dignity, trust, and responsible data handling.

💛 Privacy summary

We aim to minimise data collection, protect personal information, and design with transparency. If contact forms or early access forms are used, only essential details should be collected and handled responsibly.

Privacy overview

Mind-Haven.uk currently operates as an early-stage informational and contact platform. If you contact us or join early interest forms, we may receive information such as your name, email address, country, and the message you choose to share.

This information is intended only for communication, responding to enquiries, managing early access interest, and improving the future development direction of the platform.

We do not aim to collect unnecessary information, and we believe emotional wellbeing technologies must be built with strong privacy and ethical standards.

What we may collect

📧 Contact details

Name, email address, and any details you provide through forms.

🌍 Basic context

Information like country or general interest in the platform.

📝 Messages

The words you choose to send in contact, join-family, or early access forms.

How information may be used

Use 1

📨 Respond

To reply to your enquiries or messages.

Use 2

🌼 Early access

To manage early access requests, join-family interest, or future updates.

Use 3

🧠 Improve

To understand interest areas and help shape the future direction of Mind-Haven.uk.

Built with trust in mind

As Mind-Haven.uk evolves, privacy, transparency, and respect for users will remain central principles of the platform.
