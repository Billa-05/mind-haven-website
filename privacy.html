🔐 Privacy

Privacy matters
at every step

Mind-Haven.uk is being developed with a privacy-first approach. Emotional wellbeing is deeply personal, and any future platform experience must respect dignity, trust, user control, and responsible data handling.

💛 Privacy summary

We aim to minimise unnecessary data collection, protect personal information, and design with transparency. If contact, join-family, or early-access forms are used, only essential details should be collected and handled responsibly.

Privacy overview

Mind-Haven is currently an early-stage informational and product vision platform. This privacy page explains, in simple terms, how information may be handled.

Mind-Haven.uk currently operates as an early-stage informational and contact platform. If you contact us, join the family, or request early access, we may receive information such as your name, email address, country, and the message you choose to share.

This information is intended only for communication, responding to enquiries, managing early interest, and understanding how the platform may develop in the future.

We do not aim to collect unnecessary personal information. Because emotional wellbeing is a sensitive area, we believe digital platforms in this space should be built with stronger privacy standards, clear user control, and ethical responsibility.

What information may be collected

At this stage, any information collected is expected to be limited and purpose-driven.

📧 Contact details

Basic details such as your name and email address when you fill in forms or contact us directly.

🌍 General context

Information such as country, optional background details, or general interest in the platform.

📝 Messages you choose to send

The information you voluntarily write in contact forms, early access forms, or join-family forms.

How information may be used

Any information shared should only be used for limited, relevant, and respectful purposes.

Use 1

📨 Communication

To respond to enquiries, questions, or messages you send through the website.

Use 2

🌿 Early interest management

To manage early access requests, join-family registrations, or future product-related updates.

Use 3

🧠 Product understanding

To better understand the needs and themes users care about while shaping the future platform direction.

Use 4

🔐 Responsible development

To support the development of a privacy-conscious platform aligned with ethical digital wellbeing principles.
